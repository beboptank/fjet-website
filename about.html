Who We Are and What We Do

FJET - The Fukui Chapter of the Japan Exchange and Teaching Program(me)

FJET is an organization that helps ALTs (Assistant Language Teachers) in Fukui prefectures connect with other ALTs and local citizens.

We organize events that bring the expat and local communities together in order to create an inclusive, welcoming environment. We also try to provide new ALTs with the helps they need to get settled in their new home in Fukui.

If you have any questions, please click on the contact link to find the contact information for FJET's 2018-2019 officers.
