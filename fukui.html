About Fukui

Learn about the history of Fukui Prefecture

Fukui Prefecture

Fukui is located in the western part on the largest island of Japan, Honshu. Its capital is Fukui.

Fukui is perhaps most famous for the amount of dinosaur fossils that have been found here. The remains of of multiple dinosaurs and other prehistoric animals have been found at The Kitadani Dinosaur Quarry, located in Katsuyama. They can be viewed at the Prefectural Dinosaur Museum, also located in Katsuyama.
